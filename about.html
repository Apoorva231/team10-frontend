About CourseFlow!

This website is designed to help Concordia students plan their acadmic journeys. By using Concordia's Open Data API, analyses previous courses taken by the student in addition to historical data and load of the course and helps the student create balanced and optimized schedules that best aligns with their previously taken courses and their interests Basically we want to create a full stack application which helps concordia students get class recommendations based on their previous classes and interests which they enter
